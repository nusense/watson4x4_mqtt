WEMO D1 mini microcontroller
4 different colored LEDs
4 momentary switches
8 120 Ohm resistors

LED long lead   = anode
LED short lead = cathode

cathode of all LEDs          -> ground
one terminal of all switches -> ground

LED1 anode -> resistor -> WEMO D0
LED2 anode -> resistor -> WEMO D5
LED3 anode -> resistor -> WEMO D6
LED4 anode -> resistor -> WEMO D7

SWTCH1 -> WEMO D1 -> resistor -> WEMO 3.3V
SWTCH2 -> WEMO D2 -> resistor -> WEMO 3.3V
SWTCH3 -> WEMO D3 -> resistor -> WEMO 3.3V
SWTCH4 -> WEMO D4 -> resistor -> WEMO 3.3V
